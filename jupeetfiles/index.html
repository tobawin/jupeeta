Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jupeeta?

Jupeeta is a non-profit initiative that promotes youth development in Nigeria by incentivizing youths to acquire tech skills. Successful participants win sponsored tours to leading tech companies and events.

Who is eligible to participate?

Jupeeta is open to all youth within the age of 15-30 willing to pledge to enroll for a tech course on any learning platform.

What do I win when I fulfill my pledge?

Top pledgers on Jupeeta stands to win company tour tickets, giveaway laptops, free movie tickets, birthday cakes, branded t-shirts/bags, internships amongs others. Proudly sponsored by our corporate partners.

How do I pledge my goal on Jupeeta?

You can make your pledge by filling out the online form here Click here to make your pledge

What do I need to do to win the tour?

To win the tour, you need to improve your CGPA by at least 0.3 during the challenge period. You will need to submit your official CGPA records from before and after the challenge for verification.

What happens if I don't achieve the 0.3 CGPA increase?

While we encourage all participants to aim for a 0.3 increase, any academic improvement is a success. However, only those who meet or exceed the 0.3 target will be eligible for the exclusive tour reward.

Why we tour tech companies and events?

Touring tech companies and events exposes students to technology world, inspiring them to pursue careers in tech. It provides networking opportunities and mentorship for career growth.

How will my CGPA improvement be verified?

You will need to submit official CGPA records from your university both before and after the challenge period. These records will be verified by the CivicTours team.

When is the deadline to apply?

Pledging on jupeeta is on a rolling basis, meaning students can apply and qualify for tours and other freebies all through the year. But note, the earlier you applied, the higher your chances.
