Frequently Asked Questions

What is CivicTours?

CivicTours is a platform that sponsors young social impact innovators, first-class students, and student leaders on exclusive tours to industry events, government institutions, and top NGO facilities in Nigeria.

Who can participate in CivicTours?

Our tours are open to NGO enthusiasts, first-class undergraduates, and on-campus student leaders who are passionate about civic engagement and leadership.

How can I apply for a tour?

You can apply for a tour through our website by filling out the application form provided. Be sure to meet the eligibility criteria.

What is the cost of joining a tour?

CivicTours sponsors all tours at no cost to the participants. We believe in empowering young leaders and innovators by providing these opportunities for free.

What benefits do participants gain from the tours?

Participants gain access to key government institutions, learn from top officials, network with industry leaders, and gain insights that can propel their careers and civic initiatives.

How can I support CivicTours?

You can support CivicTours by donating, volunteering, or partnering with us. Visit our website for more details on how to get involved.

How are tour locations selected?

Tour locations are carefully selected based on their significance to civic education, government operations, and social impact. Participants may also suggest locations.

Can participants suggest new tour locations?

Yes, participants are encouraged to suggest new tour locations. We value input from our participants and aim to create enriching experiences.

What makes CivicTours unique?

CivicTours is unique in its focus on empowering young civic leaders through immersive, hands-on experiences that connect them with key players in government and social impact sectors.

How can I stay updated with CivicTours?

You can stay updated by following us on social media, subscribing to our newsletter, and regularly visiting our website for news and updates.
